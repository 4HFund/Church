FAQ

Quick answers for the most common questions.

Do I need experience?

No. If you’re willing to learn and help, you’re qualified.

Do I need fancy gear?

No. Phones are enough for many tasks. We’ll build from there.

How much time does this take?

Flexible. Even serving once a month helps. We’ll match tasks to your schedule.

Can I help without being on camera?

Absolutely. Many roles are behind-the-scenes (design, posting, slides, planning, event support).

What if I’m not sure where I fit?

That’s normal. We’ll do a quick intro and help you choose a lane based on what you enjoy.
